Our Story

Three decades of printing excellence and innovation

Since 1990, we've been bringing ideas to life

Westerly Printing Press began as a small family-owned print shop with a single offset press. Today, we're a full-service printing company serving businesses across the region with state-of-the-art digital and offset printing solutions.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and dedication to customer satisfaction that has been our hallmark for over 30 years.

Our Core Values

Quality Craftsmanship

We never compromise on quality, using premium materials and the latest printing technologies to deliver exceptional results.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to eco-friendly printing with recycled papers, soy-based inks, and energy-efficient processes.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen carefully and work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

Continuous Innovation

We constantly invest in new technologies and training to stay at the forefront of the printing industry.

Our Journey

1990

Founded in a Small Garage

Gbadamosi Hamed starts Westerly Printing with a single used offset press, serving local businesses.

1998

First Commercial Location

Moved to our first proper facility downtown, expanding our team and capabilities.

2005

Digital Printing Revolution

Invested in our first digital press, allowing for faster turnaround and variable data printing.

2012

Green Certification

Became the first printing company in the region to achieve sustainable business certification.

2020

State-of-the-Art Facility

Moved to our current 15,000 sq ft facility with the latest printing technologies.
